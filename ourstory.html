Hip Hop Performence

Romeo and Juliet is a contemporary Hip Hop dance performance that will change your perception of a classical piece of literature. It creates the ultimate entertainment experience via literature and music. We are aiming to engage young people and offering them a different view of the arts and ultimately themselves. We are giving Shakespeare a new voice and a chance to live today.

Who We Are

The cast is an award-winning dance group whose members come from all corners of the globe. All dancers enjoyed successful competitive careers before coming to perform in our show :-)
